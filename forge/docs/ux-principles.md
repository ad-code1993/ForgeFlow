# UX Principles

## Simplicity

One primary action per screen.

---

## Feedback

Show upload status.

Show parsing progress.

Show save confirmation.

---

## Clarity

Avoid technical language.

---

## Consistency

Reuse components.

Reuse interaction patterns.

---

## Discoverability

No hidden functionality.

---

## Accessibility

Keyboard navigation.

Readable contrast.

Clear focus states.
